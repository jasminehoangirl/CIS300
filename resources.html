Support & Resources

Need Immediate Help?

If you or someone you know are struggling and needs immediate assistance, please refer to these 24/7 hotlines:

  • 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988, available in English and Spanish
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a Crisis Counselor
  • Emergency: Call 911 if you are someone you know is in immediate danger
